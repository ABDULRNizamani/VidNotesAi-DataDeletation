Deletion Request

This page explains how you can request the deletion of your Vid Notes Ai account and all associated data, and exactly what happens when you do.

01  How to delete

Steps to request account deletion

You can delete your account directly inside the app in seconds, or email us if you prefer. Both methods trigger the same permanent deletion.

Delete from the app (recommended) Instant — no waiting required
Open Vid Notes Ai on your Android device and sign in to your account.
Tap the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen.
Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete account" (shown in red).
Confirm the deletion in the pop-up dialog by tapping "Delete permanently".
Your account and all associated data are deleted immediately. You are signed out automatically.
Request via email

If you cannot access the app, send an email from the address linked to your Vid Notes Ai account. Include your registered email address and the subject line "Account Deletion Request – Vid Notes Ai". We will process your request within 7 business days.

02  What gets deleted

Data deleted & data retained

When your account is deleted we permanently remove all personal and user-generated data from our systems. The table below details exactly what is erased and what (if anything) is retained.

Permanently deleted
Account & profile — name, email, avatar, streak data
Subjects & topics — all course / subject / topic entries
Video sources — YouTube URLs, video IDs, and transcripts
AI-generated notes — all notes and their content
Flashcards — all flashcard sets
Quizzes & attempts — questions, answers, and scores
Daily quizzes — daily quiz history and streak records
Chat sessions & messages — all AI chat conversations
PDF exports — export history
Share links — all shareable note links and tokens
Weekly summaries — AI-generated study summaries
Device records — registered device identifiers
PDF extraction history
Authentication credentials — Google OAuth tokens and sessions
May be retained
Anonymised server logs — error and performance logs with no personal identifiers. Retained up to 90 days for security auditing, then purged.
Aggregated analytics — fully anonymous app-usage statistics (no name, email, or user ID attached).
Legal / financial records — any transactional or billing records we are legally required to retain under applicable law.
Deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once confirmed, your account and all personal data listed above cannot be recovered. Export any notes or content you wish to keep before requesting deletion.
03  Retention timeline

When is data removed?

Here is a clear timeline of when each category of data is permanently purged after you trigger account deletion.

Immediately — on confirmation
All user-generated content is deleted: profile, subjects, topics, notes, flashcards, quizzes, chat sessions, share links, PDF exports, device records, and your authentication account. This is triggered via Supabase cascading foreign-key deletes — there is no grace period.
Within 30 days
Any residual database backups that may contain snapshots of your data are rotated out and permanently overwritten within 30 days.
Within 90 days
Server-level logs (which contain no personal data, only anonymised request metadata) are automatically purged within 90 days.
Indefinitely — anonymised only
Fully anonymised, aggregated statistics that carry no personally identifiable information may be retained indefinitely for product improvement.
